# Energy Monetization

Energy is WorkPay's freelancer application currency.

- Monthly free balance: 20 Energy.
- Base application cost: 1 Energy.
- Low rating or spam risk: 2 Energy.
- Premium job: 3 Energy.

Packages:

- 20 Energy
- 50 Energy
- 120 Energy
- 300 Energy

Purchases are architected for TON and Telegram Stars, but this hackathon build does not fake payment success. Payment setup must be completed before balances are credited.
